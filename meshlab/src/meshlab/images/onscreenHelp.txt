MeshLab Quick Help

Drag:	Rotate
Ctrl-Drag:	Pan
Shift-Drag:	Zoom
Alt-Drag:	Z-Panning
Ctrl-Shift-Drag:	Rotate light
Wheel:	Zoom	
Shift-Wheel:	Change perspective
Ctrl-Wheel:	Move near clipping plane
Ctrl-Shift-Wheel:	Move far clipping plane
Double Click:	Center on mouse
Alt+enter:	Enter/Exit fullscreen mode
